Synchronise

Meaning:

To cause two or more things to happen at the same time or operate in harmony.

Usage:

"Synchronise" is used when two or more things need to happen at the same time or in coordination. In daily life, you might synchronise your phone with your laptop so both have the same data. In dance or group performances, people synchronise their movements to look uniform and smooth. In science, clocks are synchronised for accurate timing. In videos or movies, audio must be synchronised with visuals. The word also appears in teamwork, where team members synchronise their efforts to meet deadlines efficiently. So, it means matching timing, rhythm, or actions to achieve a common result.
